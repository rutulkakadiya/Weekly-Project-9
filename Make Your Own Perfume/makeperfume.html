    MAKE YOUR OWN PERFUME KIT
    (LAB IN A BOX)


    ★★★★★(5.0)

    ISAK labs bring you a unique box to stimulate your olfactory senses and ‘Make your own Perfume’. This 'lab in a box' is a wonderful way to understand the science and art behind perfumery through a direct hands-on experience with perfumery ingredients and tools. This box will help you explore the basics in perfumery in a fun and simple way. All of this, from the comfort of your homes.

    The ISAK Perfumery kit consists of everything you need. You can make upto 20 perfumes.

    There are 12 ingredients/bases ready for perfumery along with an ISAK Lab Workbook with notes, formula sheets and space to take down your personal notes. The detailed instructions and steps will guide you through the process helping you explore your creativity while mesmerising you through the creation of your unique bespoke perfume.

    The kit is perfect for perfumery enthusiasts, experimenters, rule-breakers and those who strive to think out of the box.

    The Kit includes:

    • 12 Ingredients
    • Droppers
    • Smelling Strips
    • 2 empty 15 ml spray bottles
    • 2 labels to label your creation
    • 2 glass beakers
    • Coffee beans
    • Perfume Base
    • Instructions Manual
    • ISAK Lab workbook with formula sheets
